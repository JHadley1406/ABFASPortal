PRODUCT OVERVIEW
Goals
POD will sit behind the ABPS login and serve as a means for members to build a community around the field of podiatry, share ideas and insights, and encourage innovation.
Functionality
There are four main features of POD, all of which reside in some capacity on the homepage. Information pertaining to the members will be pulled from the ABPS CRM database.
Forum

Discussion Boards are forums where users can post comments, questions, images, and links for the group to discuss. There are six (6) different boards: Cases, Research, Exams, Residency Programs, News, and General.

Users can create a new post by clicking the “Create Post” button. This navigates the page to a simple page where users must enter a subject (which will be displayed on the discussion board) and details, and have option to attach one or more images. Users can chose to submit or cancel their post.

A user can open the details and comments of a single post by clicking on it. The post subject, details, author details, and posted timestamp are displayed. Below that information are the post comments.

Comments are displayed in the order they were submitted, with the newest at the top. Replies to comments are indented below the respective comments.
