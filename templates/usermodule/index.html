Mindy Benton, DPM

Dr. Benton received a Bachelor of Arts degree in Biology from St. Olaf College, Northfield, MN, in 1985 and a Doctor of Podiatric Medicine degree from the William Scholl College of Podiatric Medicine in North Chicago, in 1991. She completed a two-year surgical residency training program at Hennepin County Medical Center in Minneapolis.

Dr. Benton has been director of Podiatric Medical Education of the Hennepin County Medical Center PMSR/RRA residency program for the past 16 years. She also is a staff podiatrist at Allina Health in St. Paul. She was certified by the American Board of Foot and Ankle Surgery (ABFAS) in 1997 and recertified in 2005.

Stephen V. Corey, DPM

Dr. Corey received a Bachelor of Science degree in pharmacy from St. John's University, New York in 1982 and a DPM degree from the Pennsylvania College of Podiatric Medicine in 1986. He completed a three-year surgical residency at Northlake Regional Hospital in Tucker, Georgia.

Dr. Corey practices in Kingstree, South Carolina. He achieved certification by the American Board of Podiatric Surgery in Foot Surgery in 1993. He is a Fellow of the American College of Foot and Ankle Surgeons, and he has served on ACFAS committees.

Christopher D. Lotufo

Dr. Lotufo was certified in Foot Surgery and in Reconstructive Rearfoot/Ankle Surgery by the American Board of Foot and Ankle Surgery (ABFAS) in 2004. He was a member of the Reconstructive Rearfoot/Ankle Oral Committee and currently is a member of the Examinations Committee and the board liaison for the Computer-based Patient Simulation Foot Examination Committee. He also has been a member of the Credentials Committee since 2008.

Dr. Chaney was board certified in Foot Surgery and in Reconstructive Rearfoot/Ankle Surgery by the American Board of Foot and Ankle in 2002. He was an oral examiner since from 2004 to 2014.
